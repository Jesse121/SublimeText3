OmniMarkupPreviewer v1.20 Changelog:


* Add support Sublime Text 3 (Experimental).
* Add new context command `Copy Markup as HTML` (Ctrl+Alt+C).
* Remove unused command `Sweep Cache (Remove Unused)`.
* Auto scroll now works correctly for documents contain images and MathJax equations.

Available context actions:

* `Preview Markup in Browser`
* `Export Markup as HTML`
* `Copy Markup as HTML`
